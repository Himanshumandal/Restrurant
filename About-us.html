About Us

Welcome to Himchi, where culinary excellence meets a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our Story
Himchi was founded with a passion for Stable food and a commitment to providing an unforgettable dining experience. Established in 1990, we have been delighting the palates of our patrons ever since.

Our Philosophy
At Himchi, we believe that food is not just sustenance; it's an art form. Our talented chefs use only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to craft dishes that burst with flavor and creativity. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is a masterpiece, a testament to our dedication to culinary excellence.

Our Ambiance
Step into our restaurant, and you'll instantly feel the warmth and hospitality that define our establishment. Our cozy, yet elegant decor sets the stage for a memorable meal. Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a casual lunch with friends, we provide the perfect backdrop for any occasion.

Community and Sustainability
We are proud to be an integral part of the Delhi community. Sustainability is at the core of our values, and we work closely with local farmers and producers to reduce our environmental footprint. We believe in giving back to the community that has embraced us with open arms.

Our Team
Our dedicated team is the heart and soul of Himchi. From our skilled chefs who turn ingredients into works of art to our friendly and attentive waitstaff, every member of our team is committed to making your dining experience exceptional.
